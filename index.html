About Us

Welcome to Study In Europe, your gateway to achieving academic excellence in Europe. Race Educare Study Abroad is a consultancy that offers education counseling for students who want to study abroad. The company was founded in 2011 and is approved by the Ministry of Education in Nepal. Race Educare offers study abroad programs.
